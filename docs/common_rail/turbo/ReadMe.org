* Turbo
  - Engine: CJAA 2.0 TDI
  - Intended Use: Mockup/Spare parts

  The factory turbo will used. Though the original orientation will not work. The exhaust exit
  points in the wrong direction and to correct this the turbo must be flipped. All but 2 holes on
  the manifold line up. The cold side wont be flipped so a clocking pin and new slot will need to be
  created. These modifications can be done by a skilled and steady hand or milled on a machine.
  [[file:./turbo_flip_mockup.jpeg][turbo flip mockup]]

** Feed/Dump lines
   The factory turbo feed line was bent to the new feed location. This is a no/low budget solution,
   I have to careful as I've heard the lines do break. Braided custom lines are probably better
   here. The dump tubes were shortened and a coolant line that tapers on the inner diameter fit
   alright.
