# RN38 Build - 1983 Toyota Pickup 4WD

**A full restoration and modern TDI engine swap of my 1983 Toyota Pickup.**

![Truck Photo](path-to-your-main-truck-photo.jpg)

## Project Goals
- Reliable daily driver
- Strong off-road capability
- Better power and fuel economy with a modern TDI engine
- Keep it looking classic on the outside

## Major Work
- CBEA 2.0 TDI Engine Swap (from Audi A3)
- R151F Transmission
- Custom mounts and adapters
- Upgraded brakes and suspension
- Full mechanical refresh
